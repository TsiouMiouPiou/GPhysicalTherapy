Manual Therapy

What is the biggest benefit the patient will get from this? Restores movement by reducing stiffness, releasing soft tissue restrictions, and improving range of motion (ROM).

Myofascial Release Therapy

A hands-on technique that targets restrictions in the fascia (connective tissue) to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore natural movement.

What is the biggest benefit the patient will get from this? Reduces muscle tension, enhances circulation, and promotes better flexibility by releasing fascial restrictions.

Neuromuscular Re-education

A therapeutic approach that retrains the nervous system and muscles to improve movement patterns, coordination, and posture after injury or dysfunction.

What is the biggest benefit the patient will get from this? Restores proper muscle activation, enhances balance and stability, and reduces the risk of re-injury by improving movement efficiency.
